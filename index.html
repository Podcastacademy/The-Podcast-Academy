About Us

The Podcast Academy is a full-service podcasting and training provider based in Pretoria, South Africa. We offer concept-to-distribution services including training, production, marketing, and white-label podcast development. Whether you're an individual creator or an institution, we help you bring your voice to the world professionally and affordably.

Our Services

  • Podcasting 101 Training – Hands-on sessions for individuals and teams
  • Concept Development – Format, tone, branding, and launch support
  • Studio & On-site Production – Professional-grade equipment and setups
  • Post-production – Audio/video editing, subtitles, audiograms, and distribution
  • Marketing & Strategy – Cross-platform content planning and launch campaigns
  • White-label Podcasting – We produce and manage branded podcasts for you

Who We Serve

We work with individuals and organizations, including:

  • Influencers & Creators
  • Corporates & SMEs
  • NGOs & Learned Societies
  • Political Parties & Public Institutions
  • Universities, Schools, and Cultural Organizations
  • Healthcare, Tourism, Legal, and Financial Sectors
